Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Dez 27, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Transcript, an all-in-one student evaluation system designed for schools and universities. We understand the importance of protecting your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and contact details when you register for an account or contact us for support.
  • Academic Information: This includes student grades, attendance records, and other academic performance data.
  • Usage Data: We collect information on how you interact with the app, including access times, pages viewed, and other usage patterns.

How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services: We use the information collected to deliver and improve our services, including evaluating and reporting on student performance.
  • Communication: We may use your contact information to send you updates, newsletters, and respond to your inquiries.
  • Analytics: To better understand how our users interact with the app and to enhance the user experience.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • With Your Consent: We will share your personal information with third parties only with your explicit consent.
  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our app and providing services.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid request from governmental authorities.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to access and update your personal information at any time.
  • Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal obligations.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving promotional communications from us at any time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
